About

The measure of the man.

Lance Fisher is a father and a husband before he is anything else. Every late shift he took, every problem he stayed to finish, every system he built from nothing was in service of the people waiting for him at home. The work has never been separate from the love. The work is the love.

He came up the hard way. He started at the very bottom and chose to stay. He took the overnight shifts nobody wanted, carried problems that were not his to carry, and refused to leave until he understood the work well enough to teach it to someone else. That pattern repeated across every company, every role, every industry he touched.

On his own time, he builds. Full-stack applications for real businesses. Trading systems that run autonomously. Game engines for his son. AI frameworks that run on his own hardware. A nautical apparel brand. An app development studio serving local businesses. Over 80,000 lines of code across 20+ projects, all designed and shipped alone.

He is not chasing a title or a label. He is building a life that his children can look back on and understand.
